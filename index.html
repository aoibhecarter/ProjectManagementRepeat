ABOUT US

Welcome to Folláine Galway! No matter where you are in your fitness journey, you deserve to feel fantastic. We’re here for you, 24/7, with a variety of ways to move, a strong member community, and a super supportive team. On your journey towards achieving your goals, you’ll have an entire team cheering for you!

ABOUT OUR GYM

Your time in the club is you time, and we want you to enjoy it – whatever your level of experience. We start off at the pace that suits you, then take things step by step. we’re here to cheer you all along the way to achieving your goals by offering expert advice on nutrition, movement and mindset. Our friendly, welcoming gyms are designed to make you feel totally safe and comfortable. With premium, easy-to-use equipment, they’re places where you’ll want to exercise your smile!

OUR CLASSES

Here are some of our many classes

INDOOR CYCLING

Indoor cycling classes help you shed fat, improve your heart health, and boost your muscle endurance. Your legs will get a serious workout. By the end of class, you'll have a steady stream of feel-good brain chemicals called endorphins. Many gyms offer indoor cycling classes.

WEIGHT LIFTING

Lifting weights every day can support your weight-loss goals by helping you burn calories and reducing overall body fat. Although you can't spot-reduce specific areas, like your belly, strength training promotes body fat loss by building lean muscle and increasing your metabolic rate.

SWIMMING

Swimming keeps your heart rate up but takes some of the impact stress off your body. builds endurance, muscle strength and cardiovascular fitness. helps you maintain a healthy weight, healthy heart and lungs. tones muscles and builds strength.
